# Bold Path HR Website - Page Summary

## Overview
This document provides a summary of all the web pages created for the Bold Path HR website, including both public-facing marketing pages and HRMS authentication pages.

## Public-Facing Pages

### 1. Main Landing Page (`index.html`)
- Primary entry point for the website
- Features hero section with call-to-action buttons
- Highlights key services: Talent Acquisition, Employee Development, Performance Management
- Shows company stats: 500+ companies served, 98% client satisfaction
- Includes approach section (Assess, Design, Implement, Optimize)
- Client testimonials section
- Footer with navigation and newsletter signup

### 2. Company Profile (`company-profile.html`)
- Detailed information about Bold Path HR
- Company mission and values
- Leadership team profiles
- Company statistics and achievements
- Call-to-action for free trial and sales contact

### 3. Services Page (`our-services.html`)
- Comprehensive overview of HR services
- Talent Acquisition, Performance Management, Learning & Development
- HR Compliance, Employee Engagement, HR Consulting
- Detailed feature list of the HR platform
- Employee Management, Recruitment Tools, Performance Tracking
- Learning Management, Time & Attendance, Analytics & Reporting

### 4. Contact Page (`contact-us.html`)
- Contact information (email, phone, office address)
- Contact form for inquiries
- Interactive map placeholder
- Office location details

### 5. Case Studies (`client-success.html`)
- Success stories from clients like TechInnovate Inc.
- Detailed results: 40% turnover reduction, 35% employee satisfaction increase
- Multiple case studies with metrics
- Client testimonials

### 6. Resources Page (`hr-resources.html`)
- Resource categories: Recruitment, Onboarding, Performance, Compliance, Retention, Training
- Downloadable templates and guides
- Employee Handbook Template, Performance Review Template
- 30-60-90 Day Onboarding Checklist, HR Metrics Guide
- Newsletter signup for weekly HR insights

### 7. Blog (`hr-blog.html`)
- HR insights and articles
- Categories: Workplace Culture, Talent Acquisition, Employee Development, HR Technology
- Featured articles on high-performance culture, AI-powered recruitment, personalized learning
- Popular posts section
- Category navigation and newsletter signup

### 8. Careers (`careers-opportunities.html`)
- Company culture information
- Employee benefits: Health & Wellness, Financial Security, Work-Life Balance, Perks & Extras
- Open positions: Senior HR Consultant, Talent Acquisition Specialist, HR Technology Analyst, Learning & Development Manager
- Application links for each position

### 9. Pricing (`pricing-plans.html`)
- Three-tier pricing model: Starter, Professional, Enterprise
- Feature comparison table
- FAQ section about pricing
- Annual billing toggle with 20% savings

## Authentication Pages

### 10. Login Page (`user-login.html`)
- Secure login form for HRMS access
- Email and password fields
- "Remember me" option
- Password recovery link
- Registration link for new users

### 11. Registration Page (`user-register.html`)
- Account creation form
- First name, last name, company, email
- Password and confirmation fields
- Terms of Service and Privacy Policy agreement
- Login link for existing users
